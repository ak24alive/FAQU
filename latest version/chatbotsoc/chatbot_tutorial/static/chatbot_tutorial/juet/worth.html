  • Facts about the University

  
The University has a lot to offer you if you put your faith in it.
   

1.JUET is one of the top Universities in India and the best private University in Central India. Started in 2003 the University has seen exponential growth in terms of popularity and productivity.
2.The University has a prime focus on developing the technical temparament of the students and for that it provides all the support to the students which they need in terms of financial and academic help.
3.The University follows a highly transparent grading system so you know each and every aspect of evaluation in and out and also the evaluation is very robust and the curriculum is rigorous so a degree from JUET is highly acclaimed as says the analysis from the industry and other institutions.
4.We have one of the most advanced labs in the entire nation in the Computer Science, Civil, Mechanical, Physics, Electronics and Chemical department.
5. We are highly research oriented and any effort by students in the field of research is greatly appriciated and rewarded.
6. We provide international exposure for students who want to opt for higher studies.
7. We have been Accredited by NAAC with an 'A' grade which is monumental for any institution.
8. We have a very healthy programming culture in our campus to get students ready for the IT industry.
9. We have an amazing infrastructure with a 125 acres campus with its own water treatment plant, a shopping complex, hostels and most importantly the faculty is in the campus 24X7 to cater to the doubts of the students and to support them in all ways necessary.
10. We have everything inside the campus ranging from a shopping complex, 2 ATMS, a cafetaria, night pantries, 24 hour water and electricity and way more.
